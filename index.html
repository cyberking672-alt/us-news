In a highly anticipated decision, the Supreme Court has upheld the 14th Amendment's guarantee of birthright citizenship. 🔹 Campaign Finance Limits Removed

Adding fuel to the political fire, the court also lifted spending limits on political parties supporting specific candidates. This decision is expected to flood the upcoming election cycle with unprecedented amounts of corporate and private donor cash, a move that critics argue weakens democracy but Republicans view as a victory for free speech.
